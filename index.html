About us

We are a Sweden-based environmentally conscious retail-distributor company stationed in Gothenburg. We have an ambition to change an industry built on inhumane working conditions, chemicals and huge amounts of emissions. Our mission is to work with quality and environmentally friendly brands. We only work with suppliers who share our values. Long-term collaborations that build trust and allow us to grow together. The factories we work with are certified by GOTS, Fairtrade, SA8000 and WRAP, among others. Certifications that mean that the factory has undergone an independent review. We visit manufacturers regularly to maintain a strong relationship
